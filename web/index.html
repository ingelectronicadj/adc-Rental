we are ADC rental

AMIGOS DEL CINE,We are an equipment rental company for film and television, founded in 1992 with the aim to assist national and international major projects.

We have the best gear in the film industry, with the most recognized manufacturers worldwide such as ARRI, Kino Flo, Dedolight, Sony, Canon, RED, Carl Zeiss, among others, always assisted and operated by qualified personnel .

OUR SERVICES

Our portfolio has a wide range of equipment in the categories of film cameras, digital cameras, fixed lenses, zoom lenses, HMI, tungsten and fluorescent lights; remote heads, cranes, dollies, Jibs and car grip; grip and electric equipment, electric generators ranging from 6 Kw to 200 Kw and transport trucks.
